There are different reasons people start looking for a new job. For example, you want more challenging assignments, work at a better location, you want to earn more money or more security. Search It Recruitment helps people in their search for a new job. Our main focus is IT, Online Marketing and Engineering.

We always start with an extensive intake. We strive for a personal approach. That’s why we prefer to start with a personal interview. If you live too far away from our office we can do the intake by telephone, Skype or Google Hangouts.

Questions and topics you can expect from us during the intake:

  • What is your motivation to start looking for another job?
  • What kind of environment do you prefer to work? Start-up, scale-up, corporate environment?
  • In what techniques you’ve immersed yourself in the past years
  • Where do you want to grow to?
  • Depending on your background and ambition we will go deeper into your (technical) knowledge and skills.

Below looks briefly how our recruitment process:

  • An extensive intake
  • Matching process
  • Client introduction
  • Job interviews and assessments
  • Reference check
  • Contract offer
  • Relocation an application work permit
  • Coaching (start new job)
